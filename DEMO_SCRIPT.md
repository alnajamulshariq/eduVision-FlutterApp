# EduVision Demo Script

Use this script as a simple speaking guide during an expo or FYP presentation.

## 1. Open EduVision

"This is EduVision, a Smart University Attendance and Monitoring System built in Flutter."

## 2. Show Splash and Login

"The app starts with a branded splash screen and then opens the login screen. For this demo, we can switch between Student, Teacher, and Admin roles."

## 3. Login as Student

"First, I will open the app as a student."

## 4. Show Student Dashboard

"The student dashboard gives access to QR, attendance, gate history, and anonymous messaging."

## 5. Open Dynamic QR

"This screen shows a dynamic QR preview. In the real system, students can use this QR for attendance backup and gate entry or exit."

## 6. Open My Attendance

"Here the student can view course-wise attendance. The system follows a 75% rule where 75% or above is present and below 75% is absent or at risk."

## 7. Open Gate History

"This screen shows campus entry and exit activity. The first scan is entry, the second scan is exit, and the pattern continues."

## 8. Open Anonymous Message

"Students can send academic feedback anonymously. Their identity stays hidden from teachers unless a message is reported and reviewed by admin."

## 9. Login as Teacher

"Now I will switch to the teacher role."

## 10. Open Start Attendance

"The teacher can start a smart attendance session for the assigned class."

## 11. Run Start Demo Session

"The demo validates the timetable, captures face frames, calculates attendance, and shows final attendance results."

## 12. Open QR Scanner and Simulate Scan

"This QR scanner screen is a frontend simulation. It shows how a dynamic QR scan can verify a student in the future system."

## 13. Open Anonymous Messages

"Teachers can read anonymous academic feedback without seeing the sender's identity. They can mark messages resolved or report them to admin."

## 14. Open Student Gate Monitoring

"Teachers can monitor whether students are inside campus, outside campus, or not scanned yet. Gate logs and class attendance are shown as separate concepts."

## 15. Login as Admin

"Now I will open the admin dashboard."

## 16. Open Attendance Reports

"Admin can view attendance analytics, the latest session report, student results, course reports, and export preview actions."

## 17. Open Gate Logs

"Admin can view campus entry and exit records. The screen also includes a simulated gate scan and parent notification preview."

## 18. Open Message Reports

"Reported anonymous messages are reviewed here. Admin can reveal the sender only for investigation preview."

## 19. Open User Management

"Admin can preview user management for students, teachers, and admins. The form and actions are frontend-only in this demo."

## 20. Open Academic Management

"Admin can preview departments, batches, semesters, subjects, teacher assignments, and student enrollment flow."

## Closing Line

"EduVision is currently a premium frontend prototype. The next step is connecting real backend services, authentication, QR scanning, camera integration, face recognition, reports, and notifications."
